Research

My research focuses mainly on studying the geometry of moduli problems. Specifically, during my PhD years I tried to understand Chow rings of moduli stack of curves for low genuses and the geometry related to these algebraic invariants. Meanwhile, I have also developed a keen interest towards the recent theory of good moduli spaces, which I am planning to explore further. Finally, I am also working in the field of Derived Algebraic Geometry, namely in the context of Derived Azumaya Algebras and Derived Brauer groups.
